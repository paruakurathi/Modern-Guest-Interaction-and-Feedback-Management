Navigating the Diverse Sectors of Hotel

Reception

This department manages guest check-in and check-out, handles reservations, provides information to guests, and assists with various guest inquiries and requests.

HouseKeeping

Responsible for cleaning and maintaining guest rooms, public areas, and ensuring overall cleanliness throughout the hotel.

Engineering/Maintenance

Handles the maintenance, repair, and upkeep of the hotel's physical infrastructure, including HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical systems, and other facilities.

Food and Beverage

This department includes restaurants, bars, room service, and catering services. It manages food preparation, service, and quality control.

cleanliness

Ensuring a pristine environment by maintaining high standards of hygiene and tidiness throughout the hotel premises.

Security

Ensures the safety and security of guests, staff, and hotel property through surveillance, access control, emergency response procedures, and cooperation with local law enforcement.
